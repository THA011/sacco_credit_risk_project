Methodology
Data Source
The portal reads the SACCO loan portfolio from the attached Excel workbook and visualizes member credit exposures, classification, and arrears.
Loan Product Rates
Calculator rates are auto-applied by loan category. Normal loan uses 1.0% monthly, Emergency loan uses 1.3% monthly, School Fees loan uses 1.15% monthly, and M-Cash loan uses 5.0% monthly.
Member Lookup
Search using either the zero-padded member number, the raw phone number, or the account ID. Member numbers in the dataset are matched without the `M-` prefix.
